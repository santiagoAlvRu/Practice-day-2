The place where I grew up

Cordoba is the second largest city in Argentina and it has 4 million inhabitants.
However, it has a great advantage: nature is close by and you can enjoy the mountains, hills and rivers.

First years outside Argentina

Around my thirties I decided to move to Spain and spent almost 3 years living there.
I fell in love with the city and the mindset of the society. I consider that Barcelona has the best balance between social life and work.
But lately is quite complicated to find a proper place to live and not spend a lot of money on rent.






2 years of Austrian experience

Two years ago a new adventure began. New language, new goals and challenges.
That's how I ended up living in Austria and currently enjoying my life in the second district.
